Essential Pilot Resources

Pre-Flight Checklist

  • Check weather & wind conditions.
  • Inspect drone for any damage (props, arms, camera).
  • Ensure batteries (drone & controller) are fully charged.
  • Check airspace for restrictions (using DroneScene/apps).
  • Format SD card & check camera settings.
  • Confirm GPS lock & set Return-to-Home point.
